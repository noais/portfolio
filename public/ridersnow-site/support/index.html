Frequently asked

How do I check in?

Open the map, tap the "Check in" button at the bottom, pick your sport and gear, and confirm. You become a pin on the map for nearby riders until you end the check-in.

How do I appear on the map?

You only appear when you actively check in, post a planned session, or switch on Host mode. There is no background tracking — close the check-in and you are gone from the map.

How do I post a session for later?

Open the "Plan" tab, tap "New plan", pick a spot, sport and time. Other riders can join from their map.

What is Host mode?

If you know your local spot well — and don't mind being asked for tips, gear advice, or lessons — you can switch on Host mode from your profile. You'll appear as a host pin on the map. Switch it off any time.

I can't see anyone on the map.

The map only shows real, recent activity. If the spot is quiet, the map will be empty. Try posting a planned session for later, or check a busier coastline.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Account → Delete account. Your profile, sessions, check-ins, hosts and participations will be removed from our database.
