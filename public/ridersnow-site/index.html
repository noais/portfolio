Find your crew on the water.

A live map for surfers, kitesurfers, wingfoilers and windsurfers. See who's at your spot right now, post sessions for the weekend, and ride with a crew anywhere you travel.

Download on the App Store

Live — who's out now

Check in when you hit the water. Other riders see your sport and gear and can tap to say hi. End your check-in when you're done — the map only shows real activity, never stale data.

Plan — ride together

Post a session for Saturday morning, the next swell, or your next trip. Pick a spot, a sport, a time, and let people join.

Hosts — local knowledge

Locals can switch on Host mode to be visible as someone you can ask for tips, gear, or a lesson. Travellers find them on the map; hosts choose when they're available.

Privacy that makes sense

You're only visible when you choose to be. No background tracking. No always-on broadcasting.
